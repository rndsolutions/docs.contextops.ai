## About ContextOps.AI
ContextOps.AI is a managed, enterprise-ready deployment of Open WebUI, the leading open-source interface for large language models (LLMs). Building on Open WebUI's foundation, ContextOps.AI offers:

- **Enterprise-Grade Infrastructure** — Managed, secure, and scalable on AWS Cloud.
- **Turnkey RAG Support** — Connect private documents, databases, or APIs for custom knowledge retrieval.
- **User-Centric Design** — Tailored for professionals and power users, offering simplicity and advanced capabilities.
- **No DevOps Needed** — No need for hosting, model configs, or vector DBs.
- **Role-Based Access and Monitoring** — Tools for governance, access control, and performance tracking.

Whether you're exploring LLMs for the first time, or you’ve tried hosting Open WebUI and hit scaling issues — ContextOps.AI is your hassle-free path to operationalizing AI across your team or organization.
